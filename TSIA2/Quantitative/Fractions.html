📝 Fraction Operations Cheat Sheet

Adding/Subtracting

Find common denominator first!

1 2 + 1 4 = 2 4 + 1 4 = 3 4

Multiplying

Straight across!

1 2 × 3 4 = 3 8

Dividing

Keep-Change-Flip!

1 2 ÷ 1 4 = 1 2 × 4 1 = 2

📊 The "Is/Of" Proportion Template

For percent word problems, use this setup:

Part Whole = % 100

"What is 25% of 80?"
x 80 = 25 100 → x = 20

Memory Trick: "IS" goes on top, "OF" goes on bottom!
