🎯 What is Algebraic Reasoning?

The Algebraic Reasoning section tests your ability to:

  • Solve linear equations — One-step, two-step, multi-step, variables on both sides
  • Solve linear inequalities — Including when to flip the inequality sign
  • Solve literal equations — Rearranging formulas for a specific variable
  • Interpret solutions — Number line graphs, interval notation

🏆 Success Strategies

⚖️ Balance Method

What you do to one side, do to the other!
Keep the equation balanced

🔄 Inverse Operations

+ ↔ − and × ↔ ÷
Undo operations in reverse order

⚠️ Inequality Golden Rule

Flip sign when × or ÷ by negative!
Most common mistake on TSIA2

📊 Check Your Work

Substitute back into original
Verify your solution is correct

⚠️ #1 TSIA2 Trap: Forgetting to Flip!

When solving inequalities, you MUST flip the inequality sign when multiplying or dividing by a negative number.

-2x > 6 → x < -3 (flip because ÷ by -2)

📝 Worked Examples

One-Step Equation Equations

Problem: Solve x + 7 = 15

1 Identify the operation: 7 is being added to x
2 Use inverse operation: Subtract 7 from both sides
x + 7 - 7 = 15 - 7
3 Simplify:
x = 8
✓ Check: 8 + 7 = 15 ✓
One-Step Inequality Inequalities

Problem: Solve x - 4 > 9

1 Add 4 to both sides:
x - 4 + 4 > 9 + 4
2 Simplify:
x > 13
Number Line: Open circle at 13, shade right →
Two-Step Equation Equations

Problem: Solve 3x + 5 = 20

1 Subtract 5 from both sides: (undo addition first)
3x + 5 - 5 = 20 - 5
3x = 15
2 Divide both sides by 3:
3x ÷ 3 = 15 ÷ 3
x = 5
✓ Check: 3(5) + 5 = 15 + 5 = 20 ✓
Inequality with Negative (FLIP!) Inequalities

Problem: Solve -2x + 6 ≤ 12

1 Subtract 6 from both sides:
-2x + 6 - 6 ≤ 12 - 6
-2x ≤ 6
2 Divide by -2 AND FLIP THE SIGN:
-2x ÷ (-2) ≥ 6 ÷ (-2)
x ≥ -3

⚠️ Critical Step!

We divided by a negative number, so we FLIPPED ≤ to ≥

Number Line: Closed circle at -3, shade right →
Literal Equation Literal

Problem: Solve P = 2l + 2w for w

1 Subtract 2l from both sides:
P - 2l = 2l + 2w - 2l
P - 2l = 2w
2 Divide both sides by 2:
(P - 2l) ÷ 2 = 2w ÷ 2
w = (P - 2l)/2
💡 Tip: Treat w as "x" and everything else as numbers!
Variables on Both Sides Equations

Problem: Solve 5x + 3 = 2x + 15

1 Get variables on one side: Subtract 2x from both sides
5x - 2x + 3 = 2x - 2x + 15
3x + 3 = 15
2 Subtract 3 from both sides:
3x = 12
3 Divide by 3:
x = 4
✓ Check: 5(4) + 3 = 23 and 2(4) + 15 = 23 ✓
Distributive Property Equations

Problem: Solve 3(x - 4) + 2 = 2(x + 1)

1 Distribute:
3x - 12 + 2 = 2x + 2
2 Combine like terms:
3x - 10 = 2x + 2
3 Subtract 2x from both sides:
x - 10 = 2
4 Add 10 to both sides:
x = 12
Complex Literal Equation Literal

Problem: Solve A = ½bh for h

1 Multiply both sides by 2: (to eliminate the fraction)
2A = 2 × ½bh
2A = bh
2 Divide both sides by b:
2A/b = bh/b
h = 2A/b
💡 Application: If A = 24 and b = 8, then h = 2(24)/8 = 6
Literal Equation: Simple Interest Literal

Problem: Solve I = Prt for t

1 Identify: t is multiplied by P and r
2 Divide both sides by Pr:
I/(Pr) = Prt/(Pr)
t = I/(Pr)
💡 Application: If I = $150, P = $1000, r = 0.05, then t = 150/(1000 × 0.05) = 3 years
