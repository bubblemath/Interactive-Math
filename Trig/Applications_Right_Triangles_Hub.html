📏 Significant Digits Review

What Are Significant Digits?

Significant digits are the meaningful digits in a measured or calculated number that indicate precision. They begin with the first non-zero digit and end with the last written digit.

Number Significant Digits Count Reason
408 4, 0, 8 3 Zero between non-zeros counts
6.700 6, 7, 0, 0 4 Trailing zeros after decimal count
0.0025 2, 5 2 Leading zeros don't count
7300 7, 3 2+ Trailing zeros ambiguous

📐 Key Definitions

Angle of Elevation: The angle measured UP from the horizontal line of sight to an object above.

Angle of Depression: The angle measured DOWN from the horizontal line of sight to an object below.

Key Insight: The angle of depression from point A to point B equals the angle of elevation from point B to point A (alternate interior angles).
