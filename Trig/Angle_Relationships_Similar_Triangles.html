🃏 Key Terminology Flashcards

Click each card to reveal the definition

Vertical Angles
When two lines intersect, the opposite angles formed are called vertical angles. They are always equal.
Click to flip
Transversal
A line that intersects two or more other lines at distinct points.
Click to flip
Corresponding Angles
Angles in the same position at each intersection when a transversal crosses parallel lines. They are equal.
Click to flip
Similar Triangles
Triangles with the same shape but not necessarily the same size. Corresponding angles are equal and corresponding sides are proportional.
Click to flip
Scale Factor
The ratio of corresponding side lengths between two similar figures.
Click to flip
Alternate Interior Angles
Angles on opposite sides of the transversal, between the parallel lines. They are equal when lines are parallel.
