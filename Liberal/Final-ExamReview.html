📋 Formula Reference Sheet

All the key formulas you need for the final exam.

📦 Unit I: Sets

Number of Subsets: 2ⁿ (n = elements)
Proper Subsets: 2ⁿ - 1
Union: A ∪ B (in A OR B)
Intersection: A ∩ B (in A AND B)
Complement: A' (NOT in A)

🧠 Unit II: Logic

Negation: ~p
Conjunction: p ∧ q (AND)
Disjunction: p ∨ q (OR)
Conditional: p → q (IF-THEN)
De Morgan's: ~(p∧q) ≡ ~p∨~q
Contrapositive: ~q → ~p ≡ p → q

💰 Unit III: Finance

Simple Interest: I = Prt
Compound Interest: A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt)
Continuous: A = Pe^(rt)
Annuity FV: FV = PMT × [(1+r/n)^(nt)-1]/(r/n)
Loan Payment: PMT = P×(r/n)/[1-(1+r/n)^(-nt)]

📊 Unit IV: Probability & Statistics

Permutation: P(n,r) = n!/(n-r)!
Combination: C(n,r) = n!/[r!(n-r)!]
Probability: P(E) = favorable/total
Addition Rule: P(A∪B) = P(A)+P(B)-P(A∩B)
Mean: x̄ = Σx/n
Std Dev: s = √[Σ(x-x̄)²/(n-1)]
