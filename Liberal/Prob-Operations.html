🚫 Complement Events ("Not")

The complement of event E is everything NOT in E.

P(not E) = 1 − P(E)

Example: If P(rain) = 0.3, then P(no rain) = 0.7

⊕ Mutually Exclusive ("Or")

Events that CANNOT happen at the same time.

P(A or B) = P(A) + P(B)

Example: Rolling a 2 OR a 5 on one die

∩ Not Mutually Exclusive

Events that CAN happen at the same time—subtract the overlap!

P(A or B) = P(A) + P(B) − P(A and B)

Example: Drawing an Ace OR a Heart (Ace of Hearts is both!)

🔗 Independent Events

One event does NOT affect the other (with replacement).

P(A and B) = P(A) × P(B)

Example: Flipping heads twice in a row

⛓️ Dependent Events

One event DOES affect the other (without replacement).

P(A and B) = P(A) × P(B|A)

Example: Drawing 2 aces without replacement

❓ Conditional Probability

Probability of B GIVEN that A has occurred.

P(B|A) = P(A and B) / P(A)

Example: P(even | greater than 3)

📚 Independent vs. Dependent Events

Independent: P(A and B) = P(A) × P(B)

Dependent: P(A and B) = P(A) × P(B|A)

With replacement = independent events.

🔮 WITHOUT Replacement
