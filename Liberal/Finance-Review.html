📝 Unit Summary

💰 Percents, Sales Tax & Discounts

  • Percents express numbers as parts of 100
  • Sales tax = tax rate × item's cost
  • Discount amount = discount rate × original price
  • Convert between decimals, percents, and fractions

📋 Income Tax

  • Adjusted Gross Income = Gross Income - Adjustments
  • Taxable Income = AGI - (Exemptions + Deductions)
  • Income Tax = Tax Computation - Tax Credits
  • FICA taxes include Social Security and Medicare

💵 Simple Interest

  • $I = Prt$ (Interest = Principal × Rate × Time)
  • $A = P(1 + rt)$ (Future Value)
  • Interest calculated only on principal
  • Time must be in years

📈 Compound Interest

  • $A = P\left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^{nt}$ for periodic compounding
  • $A = Pe^{rt}$ for continuous compounding
  • Effective yield: $Y = \left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^n - 1$
  • Interest calculated on principal plus accumulated interest

🏦 Annuities

  • Future Value: $A = \frac{P\left[\left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^{nt} - 1\right]}{\frac{r}{n}}$
  • Periodic Payment: $P = \frac{A\left(\frac{r}{n}\right)}{\left[\left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^{nt} - 1\right]}$
  • Regular deposits earn compound interest
  • Interest = Total Value - Total Deposits

🏠 Home Ownership & Mortgages

  • $\text{PMT} = \frac{P\left(\frac{r}{n}\right)}{1 - \left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^{-nt}}$
  • Total Interest = PMT × n × t - P
  • Shorter loan terms = higher payments, less interest
  • Points are one-time fees equal to 1% of loan
