📜 Fundamental Law of Fractions

a/b = (a × n)/(b × n)

for any integer n ≠ 0

This law is the foundation for equivalent fractions, simplifying, and finding common denominators.

🔑 Key Concepts

Fraction Definition

a/b where a = numerator, b = denominator (b ≠ 0)

Three Agreements

1) The whole, 2) Equal parts (b), 3) Selected parts (a)

GCF Connection

Simplify fractions by dividing by GCF

LCM Connection

Find common denominators using LCM

Division = Fraction

a/b = a ÷ b ("3 pizzas shared by 4 people")

Terminating Decimals

Denominator has only 2s and 5s as prime factors
