The Three Problem Types
Problem Type Example Formula
Finding the Part What is 20% of 50? Part = Percent × Whole
Finding the Percent 10 is what percent of 50? Percent = Part ÷ Whole
Finding the Whole 10 is 20% of what number? Whole = Part ÷ Percent

Side-by-Side Comparison

📝 Problem

What is 25% of 80?

📐 Equation Method

1Identify: P = 25%, W = 80, A = ?
2Convert: 25% = 0.25
3Multiply: 0.25 × 80 = 20
Answer: 20

⚖️ Proportion Method

1Set up: x/80 = 25/100
2Cross multiply: 100x = 25 × 80
3Solve: 100x = 2000, x = 20
Answer: 20
