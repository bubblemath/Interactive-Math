Lesson 5: Multiplying Whole Numbers

Building Conceptual Understanding Through Multiple Representations

MATH 1350 - Fundamentals of Mathematics I

📎 Learning Objectives

  • Understand multiplication through various models
  • Master properties of multiplication and computational strategies
  • Apply multiplication algorithms including the standard algorithm
  • Perform multiplication with number bases other than ten
  • Develop mental multiplication skills and estimation techniques
  • Connect pedagogical approaches to student learning needs

📍 Recommended Learning Path

1. Models → 2. Facts → 3. Algorithms → 4. Other Bases → 5. Mental Math → 6. Educator Lab
