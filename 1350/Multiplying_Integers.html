🔑 The Sign Rules
(+) × (+)
= Positive (+)
(+) × (−)
= Negative (−)
(−) × (+)
= Negative (−)
(−) × (−)
= Positive (+)

Same signs → Positive  |  Different signs → Negative

Definition of Integer Multiplication

Let a, b ∈ ℤ (integers)

Case Condition Rule
1 a ≥ 0 and b ≥ 0 + × + = +
2 a ≤ 0 and b ≤ 0 − × − = +
3 One positive, one negative + × − = − or − × + = −

🔄 Repeated Addition Model

📝 From Your Course Materials

"If we extend this definition to integers, we can find 4 × (−5) = (−5) + (−5) + (−5) + (−5) = −20"

The Idea

a × b means "add b to itself, a times"

3 × 4 = 4 + 4 + 4 = 12

3 × (−4) = (−4) + (−4) + (−4) = −12

⚠️ Limitation

Repeated addition works well for positive × anything, but what about negative × positive?

What does "(−3) × 4" mean? Add 4 to itself negative 3 times? 🤔

This is why we need other models like the Pattern Model!

🎯 Summary of Discoveries

  • + × + = + (We already knew this!)

📏 Number Line Model

📝 From Your Course Materials

"To use a number line, let's define the multiplier as the speed you are travelling."

  • Positive speed: Travelling east (right) →
  • Negative speed: Travelling west (left) ←
  • Positive time: Hours in the future
  • Negative time: Hours in the past
The Travel Interpretation

2 × 4 = 8

+ × + = +

Move east at 2 mph, 4 hours in the future → You'll be 8 miles east

2 × (−4) = −8

+ × − = −

Move east at 2 mph, 4 hours in the past → You were 8 miles west

(−2) × 4 = −8

− × + = −

Move west at 2 mph, 4 hours in the future → You'll be 8 miles west

(−2) × (−4) = 8

− × − = +

Move west at 2 mph, 4 hours in the past → You were 8 miles east

📝 Word Problem Model

📝 From Your Course Materials

"A useful approach to the multiplication of integers is by means of stories."

The Mail Carrier Stories

📬 + × − (Brings Bills)

"The mail carrier brings you 4 bills for $7 each. Are you richer or poorer?"

4 × (−7) = −28

You are $28 poorer

📬 − × − (Takes Bills)

"The mail carrier takes away 4 bills for $7 each. Are you richer or poorer?"

(−4) × (−7) = 28

You are $28 richer!

📬 + × + (Brings Checks)

"The mail carrier brings you 4 checks for $7 each. Are you richer or poorer?"

4 × 7 = 28

You are $28 richer

📬 − × + (Takes Checks)

"The mail carrier takes away 4 checks for $7 each. Are you richer or poorer?"

(−4) × 7 = −28

You are $28 poorer
