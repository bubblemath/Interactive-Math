📐 Lesson 4

Adding & Subtracting Whole Numbers
MATH 1350 - Fundamentals of Mathematics I

🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to understand and explain:

  • The meaning of addition & subtraction by studying various models
  • Properties of addition and how to use them
  • Addition & subtraction algorithms, including the standard algorithm
  • Mental math computational skills and estimation techniques
  • Addition & subtraction with number bases other than ten
  • The inverse relationship between addition and subtraction

💡 Key Takeaway for Future Teachers

"The purpose of this lesson is not to teach you how to add and subtract whole numbers. As college students, I assume you already know basic arithmetic facts. As future teachers, you will have the opportunity to teach many students of varying learning styles and you will need an arsenal of techniques to help students visualize how and why addition works. Try to approach this section as learning the various techniques of addition and not learning the skill of addition."

— From your course materials
