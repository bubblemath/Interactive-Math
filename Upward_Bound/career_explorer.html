Find a starting point · for 8th–10th graders

Where could your interests take you?

Answer a few quick questions about what you like, and we'll suggest a career field to explore. For each one, you'll see how the math and science you're learning now connect to the jobs — and the paychecks — you could reach later.

💡

Read this first. These results are suggestions to spark ideas — not a prediction or a promise about your future. There are no right or wrong answers, and you're not locked into anything. People change paths all the time, and plenty of great careers aren't even on this list. Use it as a starting point for conversations with people you trust. The salary numbers are approximate U.S. median wages (BLS, 2024) — real pay varies a lot by where you live, who you work for, and your experience, and it changes over time.
