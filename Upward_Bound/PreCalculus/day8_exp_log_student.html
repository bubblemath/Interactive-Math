Section 5 — Solving Exponential and Logarithmic Equations
★ Two Core Strategies — fill in the method:
Strategy A — To Solve an Exponential Equation:
Step 1: Isolate the exponential expression.
Step 2: Take of both sides (or ln if base e).
Step 3: Use the rule: bring the exponent down.
Step 4: Solve for x algebraically.
Strategy B — To Solve a Logarithmic Equation:
Step 1: Condense all logs into a log.
Step 2: Convert to form.
Step 3: Solve for x algebraically.
Step 4: ⚠ Check for ! Reject any solution that makes the argument of a log ≤ 0.
