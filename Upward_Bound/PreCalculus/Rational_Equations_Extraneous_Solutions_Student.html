Day 4: Common Pitfalls & Calculus Connection

Section 7 - Common Pitfalls
❌ Mistake ✅ Correction
Only multiplying the fractions by the LCD Multiply EVERY term (including constants like \(+1\) or \(= 5\)!)
Forgetting to factor denominators first You can't find the Least Common Denominator without factoring!
Keeping an extraneous solution Always check against the original denominators—not the simplified equation.
Forgetting parentheses when subtracting Use parentheses around numerators: \(-(x-3)\) not \(-x-3\)
