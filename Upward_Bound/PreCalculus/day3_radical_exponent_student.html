Product Rule:   ⁿ√(ab) = ⁿ√a ·

🚫 What You CANNOT do with radicals:
❌ ILLEGAL — The Freshman's Dream
(x + y)ⁿ ≠ xⁿ + yⁿ
√(x + y) ≠ √x + √y
Radicals do NOT distribute over addition or subtraction.

❌ ILLEGAL — Rule Confusion
xᵐ · xⁿ ≠ xᵐⁿ
Multiplying same base → ADD exponents, not multiply.
x² · x³ = x  (not x6)
