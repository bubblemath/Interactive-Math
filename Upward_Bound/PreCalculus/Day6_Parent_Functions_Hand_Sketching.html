📖 Transformation Rules

🔄 General Form

y = a · f(b(x - h)) + k

a, b = stretch/compress
h, k = shift

📤 Outside Changes (y-values)

a (vertical stretch)
|a| > 1: stretch
|a| < 1: compress
a < 0: reflect over x-axis
k (vertical shift)
+k: shift UP
-k: shift DOWN

📥 Inside Changes (x-values)

b (horizontal stretch)
|b| > 1: compress
|b| < 1: stretch
b < 0: reflect over y-axis
h (horizontal shift)
(x - h): shift RIGHT
(x + h): shift LEFT
⚠️ Counter-intuitive!

⚠️ Remember

Inside = Opposite!
Inside changes do the OPPOSITE of what you'd expect
