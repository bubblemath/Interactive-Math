📖 Key Vocabulary & Relationships
Relationship Definition Equation Pattern Memory Trick
Complementary 🔷 Two angles that add to ° \(\angle 1 + \angle 2 = 90\)
Supplementary 🔶 Two angles that add to ° \(\angle 1 + \angle 2 = 180\)
Linear Pair ↔️ Adjacent angles on a line \(\angle 1 + \angle 2 = 180\) Straight = 180°
Vertical Angles ✖️ Opposite angles when lines \(\angle 1 = \angle 2\) X marks equal
Segment Addition 📏 Parts of a segment add to the \(AB + BC = AC\) Whole = Sum
✏️ Worked Example

Problem: Two angles are complementary. One measures \((3x - 5)°\) and the other measures \((x + 15)°\). Find \(x\).

IDENTIFY Complementary → add to 90°
TRANSLATE \((3x - 5) + (x + 15) =\)
SOLVE \(4x + 10 = 90\) → \(x =\)
