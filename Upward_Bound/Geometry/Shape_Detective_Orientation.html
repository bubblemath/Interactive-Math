📚 Quick Reference: Key Vocabulary

= Equal
Same numerical value. Used for lengths, measures, and calculations.
≅ Congruent
Same shape AND size. Used for segments, angles, and figures.
~ Similar
Same shape, different size. Proportional sides, equal angles.
⊥ Perpendicular
Lines that intersect at 90° angles.
∥ Parallel
Lines in the same plane that never intersect.
M Midpoint
The point that divides a segment into two equal parts.
