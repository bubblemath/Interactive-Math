Distance Formula (= Pythagorean Theorem on a coordinate plane)
d = √[ (x₂ − x₁)² + (y₂ − y₁)² ]
(x₂ − x₁) = horizontal distance = (leg a)
(y₂ − y₁) = vertical distance = (leg b)
d = distance between points = (hypotenuse c)
