Day 2 — Angle Relationships & Segment Addition

  • Complementary: two angles add to 90°
  • Supplementary / Linear pair: add to 180°
  • Vertical angles (opposite, where two lines cross): equal → set expressions equal
  • Segment Addition: if B is between A and C → AB + BC = AC
  • 3-Step method: Identify the relationship → Translate to an equation → Solve & verify

Day 3 — Parallel Lines & Transversals

  • Opposite sides of the transversal → equal; same side → add to 180°
  • Corresponding (matching position): equal
  • Alternate Interior / Alternate Exterior (opposite sides): equal
  • Same-Side Interior (same side, between the lines): supplementary — add to 180°
  • With parallel lines + one transversal, there are only two angle values: one angle tells you all eight

Lantern I   The Crossing Paths · Vertical Angles
Dark

Where two forest paths cross, they form vertical angles measuring (3x + 15)° and (5x − 25)°. Find x.

Strategy: Vertical angles — the opposite angles where two lines cross — are always equal. Set the two expressions equal to each other and solve for x.
Lantern flares → 7
Lantern II   The Twin Trails · Angle Pairs
Dark

Two parallel trails are crossed by a path (a transversal). For parallel lines, which angle-pair type is supplementary (adds to 180°) instead of equal?

Strategy: Use the memory rule — pairs on opposite sides of the transversal are equal; the pair on the same side, between the lines, is supplementary. Decide which of the four names describes that same-side pair.
Lantern flares → 4
Lantern III   The Old Footbridge · Corresponding
Dark

Two parallel rails of a footbridge are cut by a beam. The corresponding angles measure (5x + 10)° and (3x + 40)°. Find x.

Strategy: Corresponding angles (the matching positions at each crossing) are equal when the lines are parallel. Set the two expressions equal and solve.
Lantern flares → 1
Lantern IV   The Forked Trail · Same-Side Interior
Dark

Where the trail forks between two parallel hedges, the same-side interior angles measure (x + 65)° and (2x + 25)°. Find x.

Strategy: Same-side interior angles are supplementary — they add to 180°, not equal. Add the two expressions, set the sum equal to 180, and solve.
Lantern flares → 9
Lantern V   The Marked Trail · Segment Addition
Dark

Three trail posts stand in a line: B lies between A and C. The distances are AB = (2x + 3), BC = (x + 8), and the whole AC = 32. Find x.

Strategy: B is between A and C, so the parts add to the whole: AB + BC = AC. Add the two part-expressions, set the sum equal to AC, and solve for x.
Lantern flares → 0
