Welcome to Calculus Bridge

You're here because you're about to take one of the most interesting math courses ever designed. Calculus is the mathematics of change — and it's behind almost everything in the modern world, from the curve of a bridge to the algorithm that recommends your next video.

Most students who struggle in calculus don't struggle with calculus — they struggle with the algebra and function skills that calculus assumes you already own. That's what this course is for.

Over the next three weeks you will build a personal binder that becomes your Calculus Survival Kit. Every handout, every set of notes, every lab and project goes in here. You will leave with a document you wrote — and you'll use it all year.

How This Course Works
No grades on daily work

Everything we do is about growth and practice, not performance. The only thing that matters is whether you're improving.

You track your own progress

You'll take the same 4-cluster assessment on Day 1 and Day 12. Seeing your own improvement is one of the best feelings in math.

The binder is yours

Every resource we build goes in your binder. You take it into Calculus in the fall. It is your textbook — written by you.

A Typical 80-Minute Day
0 – 10 min Warm-up / Algebra Sprint
10 – 30 min Skill lesson with worked examples
30 – 60 min Activity, lab, or project work
60 – 70 min Notes in your binder
70 – 80 min Exit ticket / reflection
