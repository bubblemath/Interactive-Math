3

4
For \(f(x) = x^2\), find the average rate of change from \(x=1\) to \(x=4\). Use the formula \(\dfrac{f(4)-f(1)}{4-1}\).
5
If the average rate of change of a function is \(-3\) over the interval \([2, 6]\), what does the negative sign tell you about the function?
6
