🔑 The Context Clue Strategy

The HESI doesn't just test if you've memorized definitions—it tests whether you can figure out word meanings from context. Even if you've never seen a word before, the surrounding sentence provides clues!

📖 Definition Clues

The sentence directly defines the word. Look for phrases like "which means," "is defined as," or commas setting off a definition.

🔄 Synonym Clues

A similar word appears nearby. Signal words include "or," "also known as," and "in other words."

⚡ Antonym Clues

An opposite word provides contrast. Look for "but," "however," "unlike," "instead of," or "on the other hand."

💡 Example Clues

Examples illustrate the meaning. Watch for "such as," "for example," "including," or "like."

🩺 Why Vocabulary Matters in Nursing

Precise vocabulary is essential for accurate documentation, clear communication with the healthcare team, and patient safety. Misunderstanding a single word in a medication order or patient chart could have serious consequences. Building your vocabulary now prepares you for professional nursing practice!
