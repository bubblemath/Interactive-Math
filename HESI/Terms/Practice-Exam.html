🧠 Success Strategies

🔤 Word Building

Break down unfamiliar medical terms into prefixes, roots, and suffixes. Even if you've never seen the word, you can decode its meaning from its parts.

🔍 Context Clues

Use surrounding words and sentences to determine meaning. Look for definition clues, synonyms, antonyms, and examples within the passage.

🔄 Substitution Method

Replace the unknown word with each answer choice. The correct answer should make the sentence logical and maintain its original meaning.

⚠️ Watch for Traps

Be careful with commonly confused words (affect/effect, discrete/discreet) and words with different medical vs. general meanings (acute, void, dilated).
