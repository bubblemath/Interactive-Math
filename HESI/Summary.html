📊 About the HESI Math Section

The HESI A2 Mathematics section tests your ability to perform calculations essential for safe nursing practice. From medication dosages to IV flow rates, every calculation you make as a nurse directly impacts patient safety. These interactive tutorials are designed to build your confidence, speed, and accuracy in the five core areas tested on the exam.
