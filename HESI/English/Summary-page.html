💡 Success Strategy

Focus on identifying the core subject and verb of each sentence first. Use the "read aloud" technique to catch awkward phrasing—incorrect grammar often sounds wrong. Don't overthink; if you've studied the rules, trust your first instinct!

📊 Score Benchmarks

90%+ 🌟 Excellent
80-89% ✅ Competitive
75-79% ⚠️ Average/Passing
Below 75% 📉 Needs Improvement

⚡ Quick Tips for Success

⏱️ Time Management

Don't spend more than 60 seconds per question. Mark difficult ones and return later.

🔍 Context Clues

Look at how the word functions in the sentence, not just what it looks like.

❌ Elimination

Cross out obviously wrong answers to improve your odds on tough questions.

📖 Read Aloud

Mentally "hear" the sentence—incorrect grammar often sounds wrong.
