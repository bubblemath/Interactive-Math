📝 High-Yield Cheat Sheet

🔬 Cell Structure

Nucleus: Contains DNA, controls cell activities
Mitochondria: "Powerhouse" - produces ATP via cellular respiration
Ribosomes: Protein synthesis (found free or on rough ER)
Cell Membrane: Phospholipid bilayer, selectively permeable
Prokaryote: No nucleus (bacteria). Eukaryote: Has nucleus
Osmosis: Water moves HIGH→LOW solute concentration
Hypertonic: Cell shrinks. Hypotonic: Cell swells

⚡ Cellular Processes

Mitosis: 1 cell → 2 identical cells (PMAT)
Meiosis: 1 cell → 4 unique gametes (haploid)
Metaphase: Chromosomes align at MIDDLE
Anaphase: Chromatids pulled APART
ATP Production: Glycolysis (2) + Krebs (2) + ETC (34) = 38 ATP
Photosynthesis: 6CO₂ + 6H₂O + light → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂
Cellular Respiration: C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂ → 6CO₂ + 6H₂O + ATP

🧬 Genetics

Genotype: Genetic makeup (BB, Bb, bb)
Phenotype: Observable trait (brown eyes)
Heterozygous (Bb): Carrier for recessive trait
DNA bases: A-T, G-C | RNA: A-U, G-C
Bb × Bb: 25% BB, 50% Bb, 25% bb (3:1 phenotype)
X-linked recessive: Males more affected (only 1 X)
Codominance: Both alleles expressed (Type AB blood)

🦠 Classification

Hierarchy: Domain → Kingdom → Phylum → Class → Order → Family → Genus → Species
3 Domains: Archaea, Bacteria, Eukarya
6 Kingdoms: Archaebacteria, Eubacteria, Protista, Fungi, Plantae, Animalia
Prokaryotes: Archaebacteria + Eubacteria (no nucleus)
Fungi: Chitin walls, heterotroph, decomposer
Plantae: Cellulose walls, autotroph, photosynthesis
Scientific name: Genus species (italicized)
