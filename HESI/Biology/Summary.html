🧬 HESI Biology Study Hub

Your comprehensive resource for mastering the biological concepts required for the HESI A2 exam

👋 Welcome Students!

This portal provides interactive tutorials and practice resources to help you master the biological concepts required for the HESI exam. Each module includes interactive applets, key term reviews, and practice questions. Work through each tutorial at your own pace, then test your knowledge with the comprehensive practice exam.

💡 Study Tips for Success

  • Complete all four tutorial modules before attempting the practice exam
  • Use the flashcards and matching activities in each module to reinforce key terms
  • Aim for 60 seconds or less per question to build exam-day pacing
  • Review your practice exam results to identify topics that need more attention
  • The passing score for most nursing programs is 75% or higher
