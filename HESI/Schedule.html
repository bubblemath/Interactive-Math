📖 Getting Started & Data Security

✅ How to Use This App

  • Step 1: Enter your HESI A2 exam date below
  • Step 2: Select your study mode (Deep Dive, Standard, or Cram)
  • Step 3: Click "Generate My Study Schedule" to create your personalized plan
  • Step 4: Click the study links each day to access BubbleMath lessons
  • Step 5: Mark each day "Complete" to track your progress and build your streak!

💾 How Your Data is Stored

  • Your progress is saved locally in your web browser using a feature called localStorage
  • No data is sent to a server — your information stays private on your device
  • This means your progress is tied to this specific browser on this specific device
  • Your schedule, completed days, and streak are all saved automatically

🚨 How to Avoid Losing Your Data

  • Use the Same Device: Your progress will NOT follow you if you switch from a laptop to a phone or tablet
  • Use the Same Browser: If you start on Chrome, your progress won't appear in Safari, Firefox, or Edge
  • Do NOT Clear Cache/Site Data: If you "Clear Browsing Data" or use "Incognito/Private Mode," your progress will be deleted
  • Bookmark This Page: Save this app to your bookmarks so you can easily return to it

💡 Pro Tips for Success

  • Take Screenshots: Capture your progress bars once a week as a backup
  • Set Daily Reminders: Use your phone to remind you to study at the same time each day
  • Don't Skip Rest Days: Your brain needs time to consolidate what you've learned
  • Review Weak Areas: After practice exams, spend extra time on topics where you scored lowest

🔒 Your Privacy Matters

This app stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, track, or have access to any of your study progress or personal information. Your data belongs to you!
