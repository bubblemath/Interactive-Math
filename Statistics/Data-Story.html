🌍 Event-Driven Data: Global Sporting Events

Some data patterns are cyclical—they repeat at regular intervals. Global sporting events provide perfect examples of predictable, event-driven search behavior.

⚽

FIFA World Cup

Every 4 years

Recent: 2022 (Qatar), 2026 (USA/Canada/Mexico)

🏅

Olympics

Every 2 years (alternating Summer/Winter)

Recent: 2024 Paris, 2026 Milan

Compare World Cup vs Olympics →
Key Statistics Concept: Cyclical patterns are regular, predictable fluctuations that repeat over a fixed period. Seasonality is a specific type of cyclical pattern tied to calendar periods.
