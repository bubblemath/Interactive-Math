📚 Unit II Essential Formulas

🔵 Theme 1: Sets & Venn Diagrams

Union of Sets

\[ n(A \cup B) = n(A) + n(B) - n(A \cap B) \]

Elements in A OR B (or both)

Intersection of Sets

\[ A \cap B \]

Elements in BOTH A AND B

Complement

\[ A' = U - A \]

Elements NOT in A

🎲 Theme 2: Basic Probability

Theoretical Probability

\[ P(E) = \frac{n(E)}{n(S)} \]

Favorable outcomes ÷ Total outcomes

Complement Rule

\[ P(E') = 1 - P(E) \]

Probability event does NOT occur

Union Rule

\[ P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \cap B) \]

For overlapping events, subtract intersection

Mutually Exclusive

\[ P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B) \]

When events cannot occur together

Odds in Favor

\[ \text{Odds} = \frac{P(E)}{P(E')} = \frac{P(E)}{1-P(E)} \]

Ratio of success to failure

🔗 Theme 3: Conditional Probability

Conditional Probability

\[ P(E|F) = \frac{P(E \cap F)}{P(F)} \]

Probability of E given F occurred

Independent Events

\[ P(A \cap B) = P(A) \cdot P(B) \]

When one event doesn't affect the other

Dependent Events

\[ P(A \cap B) = P(A) \cdot P(B|A) \]

When events affect each other

🔢 Theme 4: Counting Methods

Multiplication Principle

\[ \text{Total} = n_1 \times n_2 \times \cdots \times n_k \]

Multiply choices at each stage

Factorial

\[ n! = n \times (n-1) \times \cdots \times 2 \times 1 \]

Special: 0! = 1

Permutations (Order Matters)

\[ P(n,r) = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!} \]

Arrangements of r items from n

Combinations (Order Doesn't Matter)

\[ C(n,r) = \frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!} \]

Selections of r items from n

📊 Theme 5: Distributions & Expected Value

Binomial Probability

\[ P(X=x) = C(n,x) \cdot p^x \cdot (1-p)^{n-x} \]

Exactly x successes in n trials

Binomial Mean & Std Dev

\[ \mu = np \quad \sigma = \sqrt{np(1-p)} \]

Expected value and spread

Expected Value

\[ E(X) = \sum x \cdot P(x) \]

Long-run average outcome

Valid Distribution

\[ \sum P(x) = 1 \]

All probabilities must sum to 1

📋 Quick Reference Formula Sheet

Sets & Cardinality

Union: n(A∪B) = n(A) + n(B) - n(A∩B)
Complement: n(A') = n(U) - n(A)
Three Sets: n(A∪B∪C) = n(A)+n(B)+n(C)-n(A∩B)-n(A∩C)-n(B∩C)+n(A∩B∩C)

Probability Rules

Basic: P(E) = n(E)/n(S), where 0 ≤ P(E) ≤ 1
Complement: P(E') = 1 - P(E)
Union (General): P(A∪B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A∩B)
Mutually Exclusive: P(A∪B) = P(A) + P(B)
Odds in Favor: P(E)/(1-P(E)) or successes:failures
Conditional: P(A|B) = P(A∩B)/P(B)

Counting Methods

Multiplication Principle: n₁ × n₂ × ... × nₖ
Factorial: n! = n×(n-1)×...×2×1, and 0! = 1
Permutation: P(n,r) = n!/(n-r)! [ORDER MATTERS]
Combination: C(n,r) = n!/[r!(n-r)!] [ORDER DOESN'T MATTER]

Binomial Distribution

Conditions: Fixed n trials, 2 outcomes, independent, constant p
Formula: P(X=x) = C(n,x) · pˣ · (1-p)ⁿ⁻ˣ
Mean: μ = np
Std Dev: σ = √[np(1-p)]
At Least k: P(X≥k) = 1 - P(X

Discrete Distributions & Expected Value

Valid Distribution: ΣP(x) = 1 and each 0 ≤ P(x) ≤ 1
Expected Value: E(X) = Σ[x · P(x)]
Interpretation: Long-run average outcome

Key Sample Spaces

Single Die: S = {1,2,3,4,5,6}, n(S) = 6
Two Dice: 36 outcomes, P(sum=7) = 6/36 = 1/6
Deck of Cards: 52 cards, 4 suits, 13 ranks, 26 red, 26 black
Coin Tosses: n coins → 2ⁿ outcomes
