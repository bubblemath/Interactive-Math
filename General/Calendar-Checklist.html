📅 Course Schedule Generator

Create interactive, color-coded schedules with printable checklists for your LMS

📋 Instructions

How to Use This Generator:

  1. Enter Course Info: Add your course name and semester dates.
  2. Set Up Course Colors: Define color codes for each course prefix (e.g., 0332 = teal, 1332 = blue).
  3. Paste Your Schedule: Copy your schedule from Excel and paste it into the text area.
  4. Generate & Preview: Click "Generate Schedule" to see your interactive calendar.
  5. Copy & Embed: Copy the HTML code and paste it into your LMS.

🆕 New Feature: The generated schedule now includes a "Print Checklist" button so students can print a checkbox version to track their progress!

Expected Format (Copy from Excel):

  • First row: Week followed by dates (e.g., 9-Feb, 10-Feb, etc.)
  • Following rows: Week number in first column, activities in date columns
  • Leave cells empty for days with no activities
  • Use commas or tabs to separate columns (both work!)
