📅 Balanced Semester Calendar Generator v4.1

Create a balanced assignment calendar with intelligent unit sequencing

📋 Instructions

How to Use This Generator:

  1. Set Semester Dates: Enter your semester start and end dates.
  2. Choose Deadline Days: Select which days of the week assignments are due.
  3. Schedule Exams: Add your exams with specific dates and link them to units.
  4. Add Unit Assignments: Paste a vertical list of assignments for each unit.
  5. Generate Calendar: Click to auto-schedule with intelligent sequencing.
  6. ✏️ Adjust Individual Dates: Use the "Edit Dates" tab to fine-tune if needed.
  7. 📊 Export to Excel: Download your schedule as a CSV file.

🧠 Intelligent Sequencing Logic (v4.1)

  • Assignments Before Exams: All unit assignments are scheduled BEFORE their unit exam.
  • Sequential Units: The next unit won't start until the previous unit's exam is placed.
  • Balanced Loading: Up to 2 assignments may appear on a single deadline if needed.
