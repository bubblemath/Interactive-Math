What is PEMDAS?

PEMDAS is an acronym that tells you the order to perform mathematical operations. Following this order ensures everyone gets the same correct answer when solving math problems.

🧠 Memory Trick

"Please Excuse My Dear Aunt Sally"

Please = Parentheses  |  Excuse = Exponents  |  My = Multiplication  |  Dear = Division  |  Aunt = Addition  |  Sally = Subtraction
Letter Operation Examples Priority
P Parentheses (2 + 3), [4 × 5], {6 - 1} 1st - Highest
E Exponents 3², 5³, √4 2nd
M Multiplication 4 × 5, 3(7) 3rd (left to right)
D Division 20 ÷ 4, 15/3
A Addition 6 + 7 4th - Lowest (left to right)
S Subtraction 10 - 3
⚡

Important Rules

Rule 1: Parentheses First

Always solve what's inside parentheses (or brackets) before anything else. Work from the innermost parentheses outward.

4 × (5 + 3) = 4 × 8 = 32

Rule 2: Exponents Second

After parentheses, evaluate all exponents and square roots before other operations.

5 × 2² = 5 × 4 = 20

Rule 3: M/D Left to Right

Multiplication and Division have equal priority. Perform them in order from left to right.

16 ÷ 4 × 2 = 4 × 2 = 8

Rule 4: A/S Left to Right

Addition and Subtraction have equal priority. Perform them in order from left to right.

10 - 3 + 4 = 7 + 4 = 11
⚠️

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Mistake 1: Adding Before Multiplying

Problem: 3 + 4 × 2

❌ Wrong

3 + 4 = 7, then 7 × 2 = 14

✅ Correct

4 × 2 = 8 first, then 3 + 8 = 11

Mistake 2: Multiplying Before Solving Parentheses

Problem: 4 × (5 + 3)

❌ Wrong

4 × 5 = 20, then 20 + 3 = 23

✅ Correct

(5 + 3) = 8 first, then 4 × 8 = 32

Mistake 3: Not Going Left to Right for M/D

Problem: 30 ÷ 5 × 3

❌ Wrong

5 × 3 = 15, then 30 ÷ 15 = 2

✅ Correct

30 ÷ 5 = 6 first (left to right), then 6 × 3 = 18

💊

Why This Matters for Nursing

When calculating medication doses, you often have multiple steps. Doing operations in the wrong order gives wrong answers—and in healthcare, wrong answers can be dangerous. For example, calculating an IV drip rate might involve multiplication and division in the same problem. Following PEMDAS ensures accurate, safe calculations every time.

Example 1: Basic PEMDAS

Basic

Problem:

10 - 3 × 2 + 4

✅ Solution:

1
Scan Look for highest priority. No parentheses or exponents. Found multiplication!
2
M Do multiplication first:
3 × 2 = 6
3
Rewrite expression:
10 - 6 + 4
4
A/S Add and subtract left to right:
10 - 6 = 4 → 4 + 4 = 8
Answer: 8

Example 2: With Parentheses

Basic

Problem:

(4 ÷ 2) + 7

✅ Solution:

1
P Solve inside parentheses first:
4 ÷ 2 = 2
2
A Now add:
2 + 7 = 9
Answer: 9

Example 3: With Exponents

Intermediate

Problem:

7 × 9 + 3 - 6 ÷ 2 + 2² - 11

✅ Solution:

1
E No parentheses. Do exponents first:
2² = 4
→ Expression becomes: 7 × 9 + 3 - 6 ÷ 2 + 4 - 11
2
M/D Multiply and divide left to right:
7 × 9 = 63 and 6 ÷ 2 = 3
→ Expression becomes: 63 + 3 - 3 + 4 - 11
3
A/S Add and subtract left to right:
63 + 3 = 66 → 66 - 3 = 63 → 63 + 4 = 67 → 67 - 11 = 56
Answer: 56

Example 4: Parentheses with Exponents Inside

Intermediate

Problem:

(73 - 5² × 2) + 21 - 3

✅ Solution:

1
P Work inside parentheses. First, the exponent:
5² = 25
→ Inside becomes: 73 - 25 × 2
2
P Still inside parentheses, do multiplication:
25 × 2 = 50
→ Inside becomes: 73 - 50
3
P Finish parentheses:
73 - 50 = 23
→ Expression becomes: 23 + 21 - 3
4
A/S Add and subtract left to right:
23 + 21 = 44 → 44 - 3 = 41
Answer: 41

Example 5: Advanced Problem

Advanced

Problem:

3(11 + 2)² - 18 ÷ 6

✅ Solution:

1
P Simplify inside parentheses:
11 + 2 = 13
→ Expression becomes: 3(13)² - 18 ÷ 6
2
E Do the exponent:
13² = 169
→ Expression becomes: 3 × 169 - 18 ÷ 6
3
M/D Multiply and divide left to right:
3 × 169 = 507 and 18 ÷ 6 = 3
→ Expression becomes: 507 - 3
4
S Subtract:
507 - 3 = 504
Answer: 504

Example 6: Multiple M/D Operations

Intermediate

Problem:

11 + 3 - 7 × 2 + 1 × 4 ÷ 2

✅ Solution:

1
M/D Do all multiplication and division left to right:
7 × 2 = 14
→ 11 + 3 - 14 + 1 × 4 ÷ 2
2
Continue M/D left to right:
1 × 4 = 4
→ 11 + 3 - 14 + 4 ÷ 2
3
Continue M/D:
4 ÷ 2 = 2
→ 11 + 3 - 14 + 2
4
A/S Add and subtract left to right:
11 + 3 = 14 → 14 - 14 = 0 → 0 + 2 = 2
Answer: 2
