👋 Welcome, Future Nurses!

These interactive applets are designed to help you master the math concepts tested on the TEAS (Test of Essential Academic Skills) exam. Each resource includes:

  • 📚 Key Concepts – Summary of important terms and formulas
  • ✏️ Worked Examples – Step-by-step solutions to sample problems
  • 🎯 Practice Problems – Interactive questions with instant feedback

Tip: Work through each topic in order, or jump to the areas where you need the most practice!

💡 TEAS Math Success Tips

📅 Study Consistently Practice 20-30 minutes daily rather than cramming
✍️ Show Your Work Write out steps to avoid careless errors
⏱️ Time Yourself Practice under timed conditions (~1.5 min/question)
🔄 Review Mistakes Learn from wrong answers – they show what to study
📝 Memorize Formulas Know key conversions and formulas by heart
🎯 Estimate First Eliminate obviously wrong answers quickly
