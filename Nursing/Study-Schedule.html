💡 Study Tips for Success

🧠 Active Recall: Test yourself frequently rather than just re-reading material. Use flashcards and practice questions.
⏱️ Pomodoro Technique: Study in 25-minute focused blocks with 5-minute breaks. Take a longer break every 4 cycles.
📝 Practice Tests: Take at least 2-3 full-length practice tests under timed conditions before your exam.
🔄 Spaced Repetition: Review material at increasing intervals to move it into long-term memory.
😴 Rest Well: Get 7-8 hours of sleep, especially the night before your exam. Sleep consolidates memory.
