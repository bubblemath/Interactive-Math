Van Hiele levels are a product of EXPERIENCE, not age. A student cannot skip levels—they must progress through each one sequentially. Teaching above a student's level leads to rote memorization without understanding.

🔑 Key Distinction: Axioms vs. Postulates

📜 Axioms (Common Notions)

General truths that apply to ALL of mathematics, not just geometry.

Example: "Things equal to the same thing are equal to each other."

📐 Postulates

Geometric truths specific to geometry that we accept without proof.

Example: "A straight line can be drawn between any two points."
