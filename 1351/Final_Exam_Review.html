Unit I Probability, Counting & Data (L1–L3)

Probability
\(P(E)=\frac{n(E)}{n(S)}\)
Odds in Favor
\(\frac{P(E)}{P(\text{not }E)}\)
Permutations
\(P(n,r)=\frac{n!}{(n-r)!}\)
Combinations
\(C(n,r)=\frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!}\)
Expected Value
\(E=\sum x_i \cdot P(x_i)\)
Complement
\(P(\bar{A})=1-P(A)\)
Independent Events
\(P(A \cap B)=P(A)\cdot P(B)\)
Mean
\(\bar{x}=\frac{\sum x_i}{n}\)

Unit II Geometric Figures, Properties & Angles (L4–L6)

Interior Angle Sum
\(180(n-2)\)
Each Interior Angle
\(\frac{180(n-2)}{n}\)
Exterior Angle Sum
\(360°\) always
Euler's Formula
\(V - E + F = 2\)
Supplementary
\(a + b = 180°\)
Complementary
\(a + b = 90°\)
Vertical Angles
Congruent (equal)
Triangle Angle Sum
\(a+b+c=180°\)

Unit I — Probability & Counting

\(P(E)=n(E)/n(S)\) · Odds = \(P(E)/P(\bar E)\)

\(P(n,r)=n!/(n-r)!\) · \(C(n,r)=n!/[r!(n-r)!]\)

Independent: \(P(A\cap B)=P(A)\cdot P(B)\)

Mean = \(\sum x_i / n\) · Expected Value = \(\sum x_i P(x_i)\)

Unit II — Geometry & Angles

Euler: \(V-E+F=2\) · Supp: \(a+b=180°\)

Comp: \(a+b=90°\) · Vertical angles are equal

Unit III — Congruence & Similarity

~ tests: AA, SAS~, SSS~
