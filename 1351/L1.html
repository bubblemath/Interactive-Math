📐 Key Formulas

Probability of an Event

\(P(E) = \frac{n(E)}{n(S)}\)

Complement Rule

\(P(E') = 1 - P(E)\)

Addition Rule (Union)

\(P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \cap B)\)

Conditional Probability

\(P(E|F) = \frac{P(E \cap F)}{P(F)}\)

Independent Events

\(P(A \cap B) = P(A) \cdot P(B)\)

Dependent Events

\(P(A \cap B) = P(A) \cdot P(B|A)\)
