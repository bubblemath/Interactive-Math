📐 Key Formulas & Concepts

⭕ Circles

Circumference: C = 2πr = πd
Area: A = πr²
Arc Length: L = (θ/360°) × 2πr
Sector Area: A = (θ/360°) × πr²
Inscribed Angle = ½ Central Angle

🔄 Transformations

Translation: (x,y) → (x+a, y+b)
Reflection (y-axis): (x,y) → (-x, y)
Rotation 90° CCW: (x,y) → (-y, x)
Dilation: (x,y) → (kx, ky)
Rigid motions preserve congruence

🪞 Symmetry

Regular n-gon: n lines of symmetry
Rotational order: n
Angle of rotation: 360°/n
Point symmetry = Order 2
Circle: Infinite lines of symmetry

🧩 Tessellations

Interior Angle: (n-2)×180°/n
Vertex sum must = 360°
Regular tessellations: 3 (△, □, ⬡)
Semi-regular tessellations: 8
Any quadrilateral tessellates!
