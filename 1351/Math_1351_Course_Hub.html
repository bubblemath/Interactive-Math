🧠 The Big Ideas of Math 1351

📊 Data-Driven Reasoning
Probability, counting, and statistics provide tools for making informed decisions under uncertainty.
🔷 Shape & Structure
Geometric figures have properties that can be classified, measured, and proven through logical reasoning.
🔺 Equivalence & Proportion
Congruence and similarity connect shapes through equal measures and proportional relationships.
📐 Scaling & Dimension
Transformations preserve or scale properties. Measurement extends from 1D (length) to 2D (area) to 3D (volume) — scaling by k, k², and k³.
