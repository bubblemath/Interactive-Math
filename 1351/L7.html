📐 Key Formulas

Pythagorean Theorem

\(a^2 + b^2 = c^2\)

Triangle Area

\(A = \frac{1}{2}bh\)

Heron's Formula

\(A = \sqrt{s(s\!-\!a)(s\!-\!b)(s\!-\!c)}\)

Scale Factor (Area)

\(A_{new} = k^2 \cdot A_{orig}\)
