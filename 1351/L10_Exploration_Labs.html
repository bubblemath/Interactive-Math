Tessellation Builder (Escher Method)

Create Escher-style tessellations using the cut-and-slide or cut-and-rotate methods. Watch animated demonstrations!

🎨 Escher's Tessellation Methods (Inspired by tiled.art)

  • Translation (Cut & Slide): Cut a piece from one side, slide it to the opposite parallel side
  • Rotation (Cut & Rotate): Cut from half of a side, rotate 180° around the midpoint
  • Click "▶ Animate" to see a step-by-step demonstration of each method
