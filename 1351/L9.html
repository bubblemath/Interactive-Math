📊 Van Hiele Progression

Level 1 → Level 2: This lesson moves students from following construction steps (Level 1: Analysis) to understanding WHY each step works (Level 2: Informal Deduction). The key question to ask: "Why does this construction work?"

📋 Key Concepts & Formulas

Concept Rule / Property
Euclidean Tools Only an unmarked straightedge and a compass are allowed. No measuring!
Translation Rule \((x, y) \to (x + a, y + b)\) where \(\langle a, b \rangle\) is the translation vector
90° CCW Rotation \((x, y) \to (-y, x)\)
180° Rotation \((x, y) \to (-x, -y)\)
270° CCW Rotation \((x, y) \to (y, -x)\)
Isometry A transformation that preserves distance and angle measure. Pre-image ≅ Image.
Orientation Translations & Rotations preserve orientation. Reflections reverse orientation.
Perpendicular Bisector The set of all points equidistant from two given points A and B.
