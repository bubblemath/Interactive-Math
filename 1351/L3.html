🔑 Key Teaching Distinction

📊 Bar Graphs

Bars have GAPS

Used for CATEGORICAL data

Order of bars can be rearranged

📈 Histograms

Bars have NO GAPS

Used for QUANTITATIVE data

Order is fixed (numerical sequence)

📐 Key Formulas

Class Width

\(\frac{\text{Max} - \text{Min}}{\text{# of Classes}}\)
Round UP to a convenient number

Relative Frequency

\(\frac{\text{Class Freq}}{\text{Total Freq}}\)
All relative frequencies sum to 1

Pie Chart Degrees

\(\frac{\text{Part}}{\text{Whole}} \times 360°\)
All sectors sum to 360°

Pie Chart Percent

\(\frac{\text{Part}}{\text{Whole}} \times 100\)
All percentages sum to 100%
